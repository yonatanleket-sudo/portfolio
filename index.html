How I Work

Product Philosophy

The principles behind every product decision I make.

The bottleneck is judgment, not execution

Anyone can ship a demo in a day now. The real challenge is knowing if it solves a problem and how to measure it. I spend my time on the "what" and "why" because that is the part that didn't get easier.

Evals are the spec

For AI, "it works" is not an answer. I define accuracy thresholds, guardrails, and edge cases before we build. At Payoneer, I shipped GenAI at 95% accuracy because we treated quality as the system requirement, not just a QA task.

AI is my co-pilot and my product

I don't just build AI features. I use it daily to query data, prototype, and speed up research. Integrating it into my workflow lets me maintain high velocity and stay focused on the decisions that actually move the needle.
